Dream Big

Apollo 13

(July-2024):

For this animated short I worked in collaboration with two peers. Our project was nominated for the Diablo valley college film festival bvy our professor. This was an especially fun project as I got the chance to brainstorm ideas and create a storyline with other artists who shared my passion for animation. The story follows a boy who has a interest in rockets, he builds his own bottlerockets as a child with his father and later becomes an employee for Nasa, following his dreams hence the title "dream big".
