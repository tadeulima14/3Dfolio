Fathoms

(July-2024):

In this project I aimed to create a realistic, deep sea jellyfish scene. I started with my deep sea envioronment achieved using volumetrics, particles, and a powerful lighting setup. Then it was onto modeling the jellyfish was brought to life with vertex weights and physics simulation effects. For the final touch I made a "distortion lense" shader and placed it in front of the camera, so the picture would be slightly blurred, giving it that underwater effect.
